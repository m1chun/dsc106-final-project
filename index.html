Project Writeup

1. What have you done so far?

I have started by exploring the dataset and identifying the main questions I want my visualization to answer. I created an initial sketch and then built this basic bar chart as a proof of concept for my interaction design. Right now, the chart uses placeholder values, but the structure and interaction are in place. My next step is to connect this layout to the cleaned data that I plan to use in the final project.

2. What will be the most challenging part of your project to design and why?

The most challenging part of my project will likely be designing interactions that feel intuitive while still revealing deeper insights in the data. I need to balance visual clarity with the amount of information I show at once. Another difficulty will be ensuring that the design still works well on different screen sizes and devices. Finally, I expect that tuning the color choices and layout so that trends are obvious at a glance will take several iterations.
